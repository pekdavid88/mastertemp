The Pegasus Story

Pegasus Hotel opened in May 1991, and is proudly owned and operated by the founding family. The hotel features 39 comfortable rooms set in a spacious, lush garden with a unique, bridge-flanked, free-shaped pool – the largest in the North of the Island – where the whole family can truly relax.

Outdoors, you will find a lively poolside snack-bar serving a varied menu, as well as an exotic pool bar where you can relax in the shade of a palm tree right in the middle of the pool. Indoors, guests can enjoy a rich breakfast in a large dining area overlooking the sparkling water, while upstairs, the main bar awaits you in a cozy, elegant lounge setting.
